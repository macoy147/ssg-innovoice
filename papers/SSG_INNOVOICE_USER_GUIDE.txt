                         HOW TO ACCESS SSG INNOVOICE
================================================================================

OPTION 1: SCAN THE QR CODE
--------------------------
1. Open your phone's camera app or any QR code scanner
2. Point your camera at the SSG InnoVoice QR code posted on campus
   (Located at: Library, Student Lounge, Admin Building, Bulletin Boards)
3. Tap the link that appears on your screen
4. The SSG InnoVoice website will open in your browser

OPTION 2: TYPE THE WEBSITE LINK
-------------------------------
1. Open any web browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, etc.)
2. Type in the address bar: https://ssginnovoice.filipino.gg
   (Alternative: https://ssginnovoice.vercel.app)
3. Press Enter or Go
4. The SSG InnoVoice homepage will load

OPTION 3: INSTALL AS AN APP (Recommended)
-----------------------------------------
1. Visit the SSG InnoVoice website using the steps above
2. When prompted, tap "Install" or "Add to Home Screen"
3. The app icon will appear on your phone's home screen
4. Open the app anytime without needing to type the link again
