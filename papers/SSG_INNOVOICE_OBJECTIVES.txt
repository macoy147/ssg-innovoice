================================================================================
                    SSG INNOVOICE: DIGITAL STUDENT SUGGESTION SYSTEM
                              PROJECT OBJECTIVES
================================================================================

Based on SSG Resolution No. 031, Series of 2025-2026
Cebu Technological University – Daanbantayan Campus

================================================================================
                              GENERAL OBJECTIVE
================================================================================

To develop and implement a modern, accessible, and efficient web-based feedback 
system that enables students to share suggestions, feedback, and innovative ideas 
to improve campus life, academic services, and student welfare at CTU-Daanbantayan 
Campus.

================================================================================
                            SPECIFIC OBJECTIVES
================================================================================

1. ACCESSIBILITY & AVAILABILITY
   -----------------------------------------------------------------------------
   • Provide 24/7 online access to the suggestion system via any internet-
     connected device (smartphones, tablets, computers)
   • Ensure the web application is responsive and mobile-friendly
   • Deploy QR codes in strategic campus locations for easy access
   • Create a user-friendly interface with clear instructions

2. STUDENT FEEDBACK COLLECTION
   -----------------------------------------------------------------------------
   • Enable structured submission of feedback through categorized forms:
     - Academic Services
     - Administrative Matters
     - Extracurricular Activities
     - General Improvements
   • Allow students to provide detailed descriptions of their suggestions
   • Support both anonymous and identified submissions to encourage honest feedback

3. PRIVACY & ANONYMITY PROTECTION
   -----------------------------------------------------------------------------
   • Ensure anonymity by default to encourage honest and open feedback
   • Protect student privacy and data in accordance with data protection guidelines
   • Allow optional contact information for students who wish to receive follow-ups

4. TRACKING & TRANSPARENCY
   -----------------------------------------------------------------------------
   • Generate unique tracking codes for each submission
   • Enable students to monitor the status of their suggestions:
     - Submitted
     - Under Review
     - Forwarded
     - Action Taken
     - Resolved
     - Rejected
   • Provide transparency in the feedback handling process

5. EFFICIENT ADMINISTRATION
   -----------------------------------------------------------------------------
   • Develop a secure admin panel for the Feedback Committee to:
     - Review and categorize suggestions
     - Update suggestion statuses
     - Add notes and context to decisions
     - Archive resolved suggestions
     - Filter and search submissions
   • Enable priority-based sorting for urgent matters
   • Support AI-powered priority analysis for efficient triage

6. COMMUNICATION & DISSEMINATION
   -----------------------------------------------------------------------------
   • Facilitate forwarding of relevant suggestions to appropriate campus departments
   • Enable status updates to keep students informed
   • Support the SSG's communication efforts through social media integration

7. EVALUATION & CONTINUOUS IMPROVEMENT
   -----------------------------------------------------------------------------
   • Track submission statistics and categories
   • Support semester-end evaluations of system outcomes
   • Enable monthly reviews to assess progress and make adjustments
   • Collect feedback on the system's effectiveness and accessibility

================================================================================
                              KEY FEATURES
================================================================================

✓ Suggestion Submission Form with category selection
✓ Anonymous submission option
✓ Unique tracking code generation
✓ Real-time status tracking
✓ Secure admin panel with authentication
✓ AI-powered priority analysis (using Google Gemini)
✓ Multi-language support (English, Tagalog, Bisaya/Cebuano)
✓ Date range filtering and sorting options
✓ Archive and restore functionality
✓ Mobile-responsive design
✓ PWA (Progressive Web App) support for app-like experience
✓ QR code sharing for easy access

================================================================================
                              EXPECTED OUTCOMES
================================================================================

1. Increased student participation in campus improvement initiatives
2. Faster identification and resolution of student concerns
3. Improved communication between students and administration
4. Data-driven decision making for campus improvements
5. Enhanced student welfare and campus services
6. Fostered collaborative community environment
7. Reduced environmental impact (paperless system)
